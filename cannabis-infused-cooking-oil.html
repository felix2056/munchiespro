Cannabis-Infused Cooking Oil

By

The steps to infusing oil are the same regardless of the type of oil used. You can even make a blend of oils if you like. My favorite is coconut oil: it infuses beautifully and can even be used as a topical remedy.

Cannabis-Infused Cooking Oil

Course: Infusion
Prep Time: 30 minutes
Cook Time: 3 hours
Total Time: 3 hours 30 minutes

Much like Canna-Butter, Cannabis-Infused Cooking Oil is an easy, versatile staple for at-home edibles.

Print

Ingredients

  • 7 grams cannabis decarbed
  • 1 lb. cooking oil such as coconut, olive, avocado, canola

Instructions

  1. In a medium saucepan, heat the oil over low heat until thoroughly warmed.

  2. Add the decarbed cannabis to the oil. Stir to mix.

  3. Continue to cook over low heat for 3 hours. Stir occasionally. The oil should not boil or simmer, although it may bubble occasionally.

  4. Line a fine mesh strainer with cheesecloth and place it over a large, heat-safe boil. Carefully pour the oil through the cheesecloth, allowing any excess oil to strain through.

  5. Remove the cheesecloth from the strainer, using gloves if the oil is still very hot, and squeeze any remaining oil into the bowl.

  6. Allow the oil to cool completely before transferring to an airtight container.
