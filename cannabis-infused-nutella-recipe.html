Cannabis Infused Nutella Recipe

Ingredients

  • 1/4 to 1/2 cup cannabis oil (start with a low dose and figure out the right amount for you)

  • 1 cup hazelnuts

  • 1 1/2 cup milk chocolate

  • 1 tbsp cocoa powder

  • 3 tbsp powdered sugar

  • 1/2 tsp vanilla extract

  • Pinch of salt

Directions

  1. Bake the hazelnuts at 350°F for 10-15 min (stir a couple of times, and watch them carefully so they donâ€™t burn)

  2. Melt the chocolate slowly in a glass bowl over a pot of boiling water (the water in the pot should not touch the bottom of the glass bowl)

  3. Blend the toasted hazelnuts in a blender at high speed until creamy

  4. Add the cannabis oil, cocoa powder, powdered sugar, vanilla extract, and salt to the hazelnuts and blend again

  5. Add the melted chocolate and blend all together until smooth

  6. Pour your Nutella into a jar or glass container that can be sealed

  7. Store your Nutella in the fridge

Enjoy!
